FAQ

You’re always welcome to contact us at support@lauritz.com. If you suspect your inquiry is of the more generic kind, here are some of the most frequently asked questions:.

How much does it cost to use Lauriz.com?

It is free of charge to be registered as a customer at Lauritz.com. When bidding, all fees will be included in your bid.

Are there any fees when bidding?

You will always be provided with the total amount before you confirm your bid. The lot price is made up of: Hammer price (your bid) + buyer's premium (22.5%) + hammer fee of DKK/SEK 95, EUR 13.

Example of a 1000 dkk bid: 680 dkk (your bid) + 225 dkk (buyer’s premium) + 95 dkk (hammer fee)= 1000 dkk.

Is registration required?

You are free to browse the site as much as you want, but registration is required in order to bid on a product.

You register here and registration is free of cost.

Once you have registered, you will receive an activation email and you will be assigned a customer number, which will be visible when bidding and shown in your bidder history.

If you haven’t received the activation email, please check you spam-folder or contact >support@lauritz.com.

How do I place a bid?

You can bid in two ways, either directly or by placing a Max-bid:

MAX-BID

When you choose to place a Max-bid, Lauritz.com bids for you each time your bid is overbid. This happens as long as there is room left within your Max-bid.

At any point in the auction you can change your Max-bid on a lot. If you do, it is the new Max-bid that applies. You can raise your bid to a higher bid, or you can lower it to the auction's current highest bid.

Example: If the current highest bid is 500 dkk and you have earlier placed a Max-bid of 600 dkk, then you can lower your Max-bid to the next bid, which in this example would be 525 dkk.

If two customers place the same max-bid, it is the customer who place the bid first, who wins.

Example: A customer places a Max-bid of 500 dkk. The next day you see the same lot and decide to bid 500 dkk. You can see that you have bid against a Max-bid, as your bid and the other customer's identical bid can be seen on the lot page. If the auction ends without you or the other customer placing a higher bid – the other customer will win the auction, because their bid was placed first.

Direct bid

If you wish to place each bid yourself, you can bid directly on an item by clicking the Bid button below the item. The will be a few steps when bidding: After clicking the button you can change the amount, you wish to bid and you will be asked to confirm your bid. The bid includes all fees associated with the auction.

Can I withdraw a bid?

No, you cannot withdraw a bid when it has be placed and confirmed. This is different with a Max-bid though, where you can change your maximum.

If I bid last minute, will the auction be extended?

If a bid is placed on a lot and there are less than 2 minutes left before the auction ends, the auction is automatically extended so that it ends 2 minutes after the last bid. In this way we ensure that everyone who wishes to bid has had the possibility of doing so.

There will always be at least 2 minutes to overbid in. If during this period the lot is bid on again, the auction is extended again.

When and how do I pay for the lot?

You can pay by credit/debit card online or via bank transfer. You can also pay for and collect your lot at the auction house where the lot was purchased.

How do I get my item?

The three main ways of collecting your items are:

  1. Delivery by post: smaller items can be sent by post directly from Lauritz, depending on size and weight.
  2. Shipment: Larger items can be shipped using our partners in Denmark, Sweden, Finland and Germany. However, delivery to islands not connected by a bridge is unfortunately not possible.
  3. Collection: You can choose to collect the item in person or lets someone else with a written permission pick it up on your behalf, including shipping partners of your own choice.

When you collect your lot you need to bring your purchase receipt and valid photo ID (passport or driving licence).

Other questions?

You can send an e-mail to support@lauritz.com or the individual auction house. We attempt to respond to all enquiries as quickly as possible. But always within our guaranteed 24 hours.
