Welcome to Lauritz.com!

We’re thrilled you’ve want to come along for the journey! This is how we’ve decided to set Lauritz.com up::

Step 1 - Explore our catalogue

We’ve got a vast catalogue of 11341 lots and navigating it all can be daunting, so we recommend using our categories and last chance options, but also setting up search agents for your most sought after items, that way you can let serendipity rule, while still getting a chance to get your dream objects.

We use cookies to help find recommendations you might like, so you never leave Lauritz.com without getting new inspiration. Just hit star on any item that tickles your fancy, so you don’t miss out.

Step 2 - Monitor your auctions

When you’ve decided to place a bid on an auction, we’ll send you an email confirmation of your bid as well as notify you if you’ve been outbid.

Step 3 - You won!

We add up all costs as you bid, so you know the total price excluding transport costs. Now follow our checkout process to finalise your purchase and choose a transport option.
