Vision and Mission

We want you to know why we’re here:

We want to set a stamp of quality and trust on the world of online auctions. That feeling you have when you enter an auction house, why shouldn’t you be able to have that when bidding on your phone or in front of your computer?

We set out to achieve three goals:

  1. The pricing needs to be completely transparent. You’ll never have to fear hidden fees when dealing with us. All fees are included in your bid, so you can feel at ease when setting your maximum bid knowing that you’ll never have to pay more than that. This is important to us, because we see your trust as fundamental in our business.
  2. To bring back the joy of discovery; We do this by offering highly curated lots and also very importantly; Giving you equal opportunity to get that one missing sofa piece or to finally see your dream chair standing right there in your very own living room.
  3. We want to nurture the budding layer of up-and-coming artist from across the world. We’ll do the curating to show you the artists you didn’t even know you loved.

We want to convey the joy of quality design and art, but we need you to come along for the ride, so we can explore the auction world together.

A wee bit about Lauritz.com:

  • We are the biggest auction house in Northern Europe offering more than 15.000 lots each and every day of the year.
  • In the year 2000 we became Denmark’s first entirely online auction house. This means we have the most experience with and are 100% focused on giving you the best online buying and selling experience.
  • We have 28 auction houses in 6 countries; Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Belgium and Germany – where sellers and buyers can consign and view our wide selection of lots.
  • We have 362 employees, of whom more than half are valuation specialists within all relevant fields, so you can buy and sell with confidence knowing that the valuation is on point.
