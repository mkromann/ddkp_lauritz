The pièce de résistance of Danish architect extraordinaire, Poul Henningsen, is the PH-lamp. Designed in the winter of 1925-26, the 3-shade family principle offers a warm and glare-free light, that gained mass appeal in Danish households in the heyday of Danish design era in the 1960’s.

Some hairline scratches on the top shade of the 3-shade family. This lamp is most likely manufactured in the 1980’s. The wiring is tested and in good working order.

Specifications:

  • Produced by Louis Poulsen.
  • Designed by Poul Henningsen
  • Materiale: Blue lacquered aluminum
  • Dimensions: Diameter: 400mm, height: 200mm.
  • Weight: Max. 1 kg.
  • Light source: Max 100W E27
