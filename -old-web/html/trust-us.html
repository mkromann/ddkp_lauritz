Trust Us

It’s imperative that you feel safe when you deal with us. A relationship like ours is based on trust - An understanding of one another. You’re generous enough to tell us your concerns and wants, so the least we can do is to open up and let you know a bit about us.

  • That’s why we operate with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
  • It’s why we set up a guarantee when you contact us. You need to know we’ll reply to you within 24 hours, because that’s common decency.
  • That along with our initiative to eradicate fake bidders. We’ve seen evidence of 0.5% of new accounts being fake and we want to get that number down to 0%. That’s why bidder history is now available for anyone to see.

Police Approval

We’ve been granted access to the Police’s register of stolen goods, so we check that all items are safe and ready for bidding. It’s a rigorous process to sell through us. We demand identification to ensure that our customers can sleep safely knowing that everything is on the level.

Authenticity

You need to know that you’re bidding on authentic goods. That’s why we offer a stamp of authenticity. It’s human to error, so we do make mistakes, but we know want to look for and we’re thorough! You can feel safe, that you are dealing with a professional business, so in the case any mistakes are made, they will be dealt with in a professional manner.

Your information is safe with us

When you’ve logged in, it’s nice and easy for you to bid with confidence, because our vault with your information is secured by some of the programmers in the business.
